What Is AI?

A method of making a computer or a software think intelligently like the human mind.

The goal for AI is to be able to do things such as recognize patterns, make decisions, and judge like humans.

Are Humans Replaceable ?

(click the image for detailed info)

Generative AI, a rising star, can create human-like text, graphics, summarise scientific articles, debug code, execute commands like crafting Microsoft Excel formulas and much more!
The entry of OpenAI's ChatGPT and Google's Bard hasignited fierce competition, ushering in a new era of innovation in the field of AI.
A Goldman Sachs report suggests that generative AI could automate 18% of global work, impacting approximately 300 million jobs, with advanced economies facing a higher impact.
In the U.S. and Europe, two-thirds of jobs areexpected to be exposed to some degree of AI automation, and around 25% of all jobs might be entirely performed by AI.
Globally, the study reveals the potential automation of the workforce, with certain countries, including the U.S., U.K., Japan, and Hong Kong, facing rates exceeding 28%.

How Will The Work Environment Look In The Near Future ?

(click the image for detailed info)

Public Concerns:
A Forbes survey reveals that 77% of Americans express worry about AI causing job losses, reflecting widespread concerns among the population.
WEF Jobs Outlook:
Contrary to fears of mass unemployment, the World Economic Forum's 2020 Jobs Report predicts a net gain, anticipating the creation of 97 million jobs alongside the displacement of 85 million due to AI.
LLM Challenges:
ChatGPT brings misinformation challenges, requiring regular fact-checking and human oversight.
Judgment and AI:
LLMs, due to their inherent limitations, demonstrate a lack of independent judgment.
This underscores the critical necessity for human supervision when utilizing such models to ensure responsible and contextually appropriate outcomes.
Strategic Upskilling:
Continual upskilling ensures job security by overcoming AI limitations. AI's productivity focus empowers professionals to concentrate on high-value tasks in roles like graphic design and software development.
